## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 500 g minced beef
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 cup red wine
- 800 g crushed tomatoes
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 bay leaf
- 200 g mushrooms, sliced
- 1/2 cup grated parmesan
- 400 g pasta of choice
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Preparation

1. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes until soft and translucent.
3. Stir in the garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
4. Add the minced beef and mushrooms, breaking up the meat as it browns, about 8 minutes.
5. Stir in the tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes.
6. Pour in the red wine and simmer until reduced by half, about 5 minutes.
7. Add the crushed tomatoes, oregano, and bay leaf. Season with salt and pepper.
8. Simmer uncovered on low heat for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
9. Meanwhile, cook the pasta in salted boiling water according to the package instructions.
10. Remove the bay leaf, stir in the grated parmesan, and adjust seasoning.
11. Toss the sauce through the pasta and serve immediately.
