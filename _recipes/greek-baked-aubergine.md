## Ingredients

- 2 large aubergines, sliced into rounds
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 400 g crushed tomatoes
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 100 g feta, crumbled
- 12 kalamata olives, pitted
- 200 g ricotta
- 1 egg
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh basil, to serve

## Preparation

1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (390°F).
2. Arrange the aubergine slices on a baking tray, brush with 2 tbsp olive oil, and roast for 20 minutes until golden.
3. Meanwhile, heat the remaining olive oil in a pan and sauté the onion for 5 minutes.
4. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute, then stir in the crushed tomatoes and oregano.
5. Simmer the tomato sauce for 10 minutes, then season with salt and pepper.
6. In a bowl, mix the ricotta with the egg and half the feta.
7. In a baking dish, layer the roasted aubergine, tomato sauce, ricotta mixture, and olives, repeating until all ingredients are used.
8. Top with the remaining feta and bake for 20 minutes until bubbling and golden.
9. Scatter with fresh basil before serving.
