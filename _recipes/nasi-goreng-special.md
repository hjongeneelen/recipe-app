## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 red chili, sliced (optional)
- 150 g chicken breast, diced
- 1 carrot, diced
- 100 g green beans, chopped
- 3 cups cooked rice, cold (day-old works best)
- 2 tbsp kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp shrimp paste (terasi), optional
- 2 spring onions, sliced
- Prawn crackers, to serve

## Preparation

1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok or large frying pan and fry the eggs sunny-side up. Set aside.
2. Add the remaining oil to the wok and sauté the onion, garlic, and chili for 2 minutes.
3. Add the chicken breast and cook until no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
4. Stir in the carrot and green beans, cooking for 3 minutes until just tender.
5. Add the cold rice, breaking up any clumps, and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
6. Stir in the kecap manis, soy sauce, and shrimp paste until evenly coated.
7. Toss through half the spring onions.
8. Divide between plates, top each with a fried egg, and garnish with remaining spring onions and prawn crackers.
